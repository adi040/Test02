Conversion of Marks to Percentage for Joint Courses

Why?

Internal lab practical marks can't be determined, so marks can be calculated only by percentage value.

For Theory Course directly enter your scores in marks.

Calculations

  1. Result: Result = Total marks / Weightage
  2. Scored percentage: Scored mark / Result

Example:

  • Total Marks = 25

    Scored Marks = 24

    Weightage(%) = 10

    Result: 25 / 10 = 2.5

    Scored percentage: 24 / 2.5 = 9.6%

* Disclaimer: Marks must be converted to percentages before calculations for joint courses.
